# **App Name**: Aurelian Aesthetics

## Core Features:

- Services Showcase: Detailed descriptions of hair patches, hair transplants, cosmetic procedures, and bridal/groom packages.
- Before-and-After Gallery: Interactive gallery to showcase the results of various cosmetic procedures with image uploads. Categorize the images and the category buttons must be prominent.
- Online Appointment Booking: Form for clients to book appointments online with date and time pickers, including field validation and user notification upon submission.
- Client Testimonials: Display of client testimonials, optionally with images, in a visually appealing carousel or grid layout.
- FAQ Section: Dedicated section addressing frequently asked questions regarding services and procedures. Each question should have a short, AI generated summary to give a user some quick reasoning as to whether the full answer might be useful to them. LLM as tool
- Bottom Sticky Navigation: A persistent navigation bar fixed to the bottom of the screen for easy access to key sections, especially useful on mobile devices. Links to the different sections in the page.
- Footer: Contains important information such as contact details, business address, copyright notice, and social media links.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Soft blush (#F8E7E1) for a gentle, inviting feel.
- Background color: Clean white (#FAFAFA) for a crisp, professional look with gold accents. (#E4C499).
- Accent color: Pale gold (#E2D8CE), for highlights and calls to action; visually harmonious with the analogous soft blush color but with slightly different saturation and brightness for appropriate contrast.
- Body font: 'Alegreya', serif for an elegant and intellectual style. Headline font: 'Belleza' a sans-serif with an art and fashion style, for short impactful amounts of text.
- Elegant, minimalist icons for services and navigation. Designed to have high contrast.
- Mobile-first, fully responsive design adapting seamlessly to different screen sizes (phones, tablets, PCs).
- Subtle transition effects and animations to enhance user experience.
